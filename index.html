PlanetBuddy

Your Eco-Friendly Companion

Track, improve, and save the planet with PlanetBuddy. Empower individuals to adopt eco-friendly habits and contribute to a sustainable future.

Problem Statement

Many people are unaware of the impact their daily habits have on the environment. There is a lack of accessible tools to track and improve eco-friendly behavior.

Solution

PlanetBuddy provides habit tracking, eco-score calculations, and personalized tips to encourage sustainable choices.

Key Features

  • Habit Tracker: Input daily habits like energy use, plastic consumption, and transportation choices.
  • Eco-Score: Calculate a score based on user input using JavaScript.
  • Personalized Tips: Suggest ways to improve eco-scores and habits.

Target Audience & Impact

Audience: Eco-conscious individuals, students, and families.

Impact: Promote awareness and encourage small, meaningful steps towards a sustainable lifestyle.
